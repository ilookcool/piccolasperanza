About Us


Piccola Speranza is a Portuguese brand for children's clothing that makes the diference by the beauty, quality, styling and confort of its clothes.


Conceived, designed and produced in Portugal, Piccola Speranza will continue presenting their collections privileging the mix of exclusive design, produced with quality at a competitive price.


Piccola Speranza knows that children have dreams full of fantasy and beauty, and fulfilling those dreams, cherishing their fairytale becomes a very rewarding challenge.


Piccola Speranza collections showing up-to-date fashion, using high quality fabrics and accessories to match, highlighting the glamour of the finished product.


A Child or a Youth wearing any Piccola Speranza model will always feel herself admired by everybody around.


Children and Youths want to feel themselves happy and Piccola Speranza with creativity will ensure that expected happiness.
