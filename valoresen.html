Our Values


The values ​​that guide the brand Piccola Speranza can be defined as:


Responsibility - We do not ignore the importance that the firsts costumes represents for the human being. We are pioneers: before an collegian costume, uniform or apparel, indeed our clothes were there;


Passion - After the responsibility of doing the perfect clothing we have passion to do it in the best way, that it the only path to commit ourselves with perfectionism;


Sustainability - It is our strong desire that children and young people today enjoy a harmonious and sustainable world in the future. With this plan we have the pleasure of protect the environment with a responsible attitude;


Commitment – We are committed to these values ​​and, ultimately, to create value in society.
